Jeremy Ash

Bio

Jeremy Ashs research is at the interface of cheminformatics, bioinformatics, and data science. He is an experienced software developer with a concentration on machine learning methods and user friendly software for the analysis of chemical data. Jeremy started off in bioinformatics, so he has a special interest in combining bioinformatics and cheminformatics methodologies. His dissertation was on statistical methods development for QSAR models models predicting the bioactivity of small molecules given their chemical structure.

Education

North Carolina State University | Raleigh, NC

Ph. D. in Bioinformatics | M. S. in Statistics

The University of Texas, Austin | Austin, TX

B.S. in Biology

Experience

SAS | Research Statistician in JMP R&D | January 2020 - Present
